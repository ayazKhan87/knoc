Account Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the Knoc app
  2. Open the drawer menu in the home screen
  3. Navigate to Profile section
  4. Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" option
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted

Data That Will Be Permanently Deleted:

For Users:

  • Name
  • Contact number
  • FCM token
  • Profile pictures
  • Device information

For Shops:

  • Contact number
  • FCM token
  • Shop verification photos
  • Profile pictures
  • Device information

Data We Retain:

For Users:

  • Email address
  • Product inquiry history with location data
  • Search location history

For Shops:

  • Email address
  • Shop name
  • Shop owner name
  • Shop category
  • Registration location

This data is retained for market analysis, improving local product discovery, and maintaining business category insights.
